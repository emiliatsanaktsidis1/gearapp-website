Gear – Account Suspension and Banning Policy

Last updated: 13 December 2025

Gear may restrict, suspend, or permanently ban accounts to protect users, prevent fraud, and enforce our policies.

1. Reasons for Suspension or Ban

Your account may be limited, suspended, or permanently banned if you:

  • Engage in fraud, scams, or dishonest behaviour;
  • Repeatedly violate Gear policies or Community Guidelines;
  • List prohibited or illegal items;
  • Abuse chargebacks or dispute systems;
  • Fail to ship items after accepting payment;
  • Trigger serious concerns in connection with Stripe’s risk checks or identity verification.

2. Possible Actions

  • Removal of listings;
  • Temporary account suspension (unable to buy, sell, or message);
  • Permanent ban;
  • Holding or delaying payouts while investigations are underway;
  • Blocking account deletion during active disputes or investigations.

3. Appeals

If you believe your account was restricted in error, you can contact Gear with relevant details and supporting evidence. Gear will review the information and make a final decision.
