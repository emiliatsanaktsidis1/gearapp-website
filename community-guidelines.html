Gear – Community Guidelines

Last updated: 13 December 2025

Gear aims to be a respectful, safe marketplace. These guidelines apply to listings, messaging, and all user interactions.

1. Respect Other Users

  • Be polite and professional.
  • No harassment, hate speech, threats, or abusive behaviour.
  • No discriminatory content.

2. Be Honest and Transparent

  • Accurately represent items, including condition and defects.
  • Do not mislead others about authenticity, fitment, or performance.
  • Use genuine photos of the item for sale.

3. No Scams or Fraud

  • No fake listings or items you do not own.
  • No fake tracking numbers or empty packages.
  • No attempts to trick or deceive buyers or sellers.

4. Keep Transactions On-Platform

  • Do not request or accept payment outside Gear (e.g., PayID, direct transfer) for Gear listings.
  • Do not offer discounts to move payments off-platform.
  • Off-platform payments remove protections and may result in account removal.

5. Safety Tips

  • If meeting in person, choose a public location and use common-sense safety precautions.
  • Do not share unnecessary sensitive personal information.
  • Report suspicious behaviour to Gear.
