Gear – Fees and Payout Policy

Last updated: 13 December 2025

This policy explains Gear’s marketplace fee, payment processing, and seller payouts.

1. Marketplace Fee

Gear charges a 10% fee on the total sale amount, including the item price and shipping. This fee is automatically deducted from the buyer’s payment. Marketplace fees are generally non-refundable.

2. Payment Processing

All payments are processed via Stripe. Sellers must complete Stripe onboarding and link an adult-owned payout account to receive funds.

3. Payout Timing

Payout timing depends on Stripe’s processing timelines and risk checks. In some cases, payouts may be delayed.

4. Holds, Disputes, and Chargebacks

  • If there are disputes, chargebacks, or fraud concerns, Gear may hold payouts until the issue is resolved.
  • Chargebacks may reverse funds from the seller’s Stripe balance and may incur dispute fees charged by Stripe.
  • If Gear determines fraud occurred, Gear may refund the buyer and reverse or withhold the seller’s payout.

5. Negative Balances

If a seller’s balance becomes negative due to refunds, chargebacks, or fees, the seller is responsible for covering that negative balance as required by Stripe and applicable payment rules.
