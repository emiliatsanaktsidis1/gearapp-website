Gear – Listing Rules and Quality Standards

Last updated: 13 December 2025

These rules help keep listings accurate, trustworthy, and useful for buyers.

1. Photos

  • Use clear, well-lit photos of the actual item for sale.
  • Do not use stolen or unauthorised images from other websites or sellers.
  • Avoid edits that materially misrepresent the item.

2. Descriptions

  • Include the part name, condition (new/used/reconditioned), and key details.
  • Disclose known defects, damage, missing components, or repairs.
  • Do not make false claims about authenticity or performance.

3. Pricing and Shipping

  • Set honest pricing and shipping costs.
  • Do not hide extra charges in the description.
  • Do not request additional payment outside the App.

4. Spam and Duplicate Listings

  • Do not post the same item repeatedly to gain extra visibility.
  • Do not post irrelevant or misleading listings.

5. Fitment and Compatibility

If you provide fitment/compatibility information, you must do so in good faith and to the best of your knowledge. Misrepresenting fitment to increase sales is prohibited.
