Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gear ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Gear, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account information may include your name, email address, phone number, password (stored securely), profile photo (optional), and date of birth (for eligibility checks).

Listing information may include item descriptions and photos, vehicle/compatibility information, pricing, and approximate location information.

Transaction information may include shipping and delivery addresses, purchase and sales history, and messages between buyers and sellers.

Auction and bidding information (where enabled) may include bid amounts, bid timestamps, bid history for a listing, auction end times, anti-sniping extensions, and auction payment deadlines.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique identifiers);
  • IP address and general location (e.g., city/region);
  • App usage data (pages viewed, buttons tapped, time spent);
  • Search queries and browsing activity within the App;
  • Crash logs and performance data.

2.3 Payment Information (via Stripe)

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store full credit card numbers on our servers. Stripe provides us with limited payment information (such as card type, last four digits, and payment status) so that we can confirm payments, manage orders, and process payouts.

2.4 Information From Third Parties

  • Identity and verification information from payment providers (e.g., Stripe) when required for payouts;
  • Optional vehicle or part information from third-party lookup or reference services;
  • Information from analytics and crash-reporting tools (e.g., Firebase).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your Gear account;
  • Enable buying and selling of car parts between users;
  • Facilitate in-app messaging, notifications, and order updates;
  • Process payments and payouts via Stripe;
  • Operate auctions and bidding features (including anti-sniping extensions and payment deadlines);
  • Provide customer support and respond to enquiries;
  • Analyse how the App is used to improve functionality and performance;
  • Monitor for and detect fraud, scams, and suspicious activity;
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

Your public profile information (such as username, profile photo, general location, and listings) may be visible to other users. Buyers and sellers may see each other's usernames and relevant order details for a transaction. For auctions, we may display listing-level auction information such as current bid, bid count, and time remaining. We do not intend to publicly display your full identity as a bidder beyond what is necessary to operate the transaction.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties that help us operate the App, including Stripe (payments, risk checks, and identity verification), Firebase/Google Cloud (authentication, database, storage, analytics, and crash reporting), email and SMS providers, and analytics providers. These providers only access your information to perform services on our behalf and are required to protect it appropriately.

4.3 For Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose information where required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Gear, our users, or others, and to detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), access controls, secure authentication mechanisms, and regular monitoring. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax, financial reporting, and regulatory obligations. Auction and bid records may be retained to operate the service, handle disputes, and prevent fraud.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can access and update much of your account information in the App.
  • You may request deletion of your account, subject to legal and operational requirements (including fraud and dispute investigations).
  • You can opt out of promotional emails (transactional/service messages may still be sent).
