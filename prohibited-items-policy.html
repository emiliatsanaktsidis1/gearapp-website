Gear – Prohibited Items Policy

Last updated: 13 December 2025

To keep Gear safe and lawful, certain items cannot be listed or sold. Listings that violate this policy may be removed and accounts may be restricted or banned.

1. Illegal or Restricted Items

  • Stolen parts or items you do not have the legal right to sell;
  • VIN plates, chassis numbers, or vehicle identifier components;
  • Airbags, pyrotechnic seat belt pretensioners, or any explosive components;
  • Items requiring a licence to sell where the seller does not hold that licence;
  • Any item illegal to sell in the seller’s or buyer’s jurisdiction.

2. Dangerous Goods and Contaminated Items

  • Fuel tanks containing fuel or vapour;
  • Hazardous chemicals, flammables, or regulated substances;
  • Items contaminated with asbestos or hazardous residues;
  • Used oil filters or waste products.

3. Counterfeit Goods

  • Fake branded parts;
  • Replica parts sold as genuine OEM or branded items;
  • Items that infringe trademarks or intellectual property.

4. Non-Automotive Items

Gear is dedicated to automotive parts and accessories. Non-automotive items are prohibited, including general household goods, cosmetics, animals, or unrelated electronics.

5. Fraudulent Listings

  • Listings using stolen photos;
  • Listings for items not owned or not available;
  • Listings designed to scam buyers or circumvent fees.
