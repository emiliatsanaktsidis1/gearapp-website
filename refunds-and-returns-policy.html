Gear – Refunds and Returns Policy

Last updated: 13 December 2025

Gear is a marketplace for car parts. All sales are final except in cases of verified fraud. This policy explains when a refund may be considered.

1. All Sales Are Final

Unless otherwise required by law, purchases made through Gear are final. Gear does not offer returns or refunds for:

  • Change of mind;
  • Fitment or compatibility issues (buyers must check suitability before purchasing);
  • Minor condition disputes where the listing was generally accurate;
  • Courier delays or missed delivery attempts;
  • Damage caused in transit by the courier.

2. Fraud-Based Refunds

Gear may issue a refund only where we determine that fraud has occurred. Examples include:

  • The seller never shipped the item and cannot provide valid proof of shipment;
  • The seller provided clearly fake or invalid tracking information;
  • The item received is completely different from what was advertised (e.g., empty box, unrelated item);
  • The item is clearly counterfeit but was listed as genuine.

3. Reporting a Problem

If you believe you have been the victim of fraud, contact Gear as soon as possible and provide:

  • Your order number and listing details;
  • Photos of the item and packaging (if received);
  • Relevant messages between buyer and seller;
  • Any tracking information or courier evidence.

4. Investigation and Outcomes

Gear may review listing details, messages, tracking, account history, and other available evidence. Based on our review, Gear may:

  • Refund the buyer and reverse or withhold the seller’s payout;
  • Deny the refund request;
  • Restrict or suspend an account for policy breaches.

Gear’s decision is final.
