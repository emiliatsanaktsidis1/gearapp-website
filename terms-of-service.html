Gear charges a 10% marketplace fee on the total sale amount, including item price and shipping. This fee is automatically deducted at the time of sale and is non-refundable.

Payments are processed by Stripe. By using Gear, you also agree to Stripe's terms and policies. Gear does not store complete credit card numbers. We receive tokens and limited payment data from Stripe to facilitate orders and payouts.

6. Seller Payouts

6.1 Payout Setup Requirement

To receive payouts for sales, sellers must complete payout setup through Stripe Connect. If you receive a sale before completing payout setup, you will have 72 hours to complete setup. If payout setup is not completed within this window, the order will be automatically cancelled and the buyer will be refunded.

6.2 Marketplace Fee

Gear charges a 10% marketplace fee on the total sale amount (item price plus shipping). This fee is automatically deducted from your payout. For example, if you sell an item for $100 with $10 shipping, your payout will be $99 (90% of $110).

6.3 Payout Holds & Release Schedule

To protect buyers and maintain marketplace integrity, seller payouts are held until fulfilment is confirmed. Payouts may also be subject to additional verification checks by Stripe or Gear before release.

  • Tracked shipments: Payout is released 3 business days after tracking shows delivered. If tracking does not show delivered within 14 days, the payout will be released automatically.
  • Untracked shipments: Payout is released 10 business days after the seller marks the order as shipped.

Business days exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and are calculated based on Australian Eastern Time.

6.4 Disputes and Refunds

If an order is disputed or refunded before the payout is released, the pending payout will be voided. Gear reserves the right to hold or delay payouts if there is suspected fraud, policy violations, or ongoing disputes.

6.5 Payout Processing

Once a payout is released, it will be transferred to your connected Stripe account. The time it takes to reach your bank account depends on your bank and Stripe's processing times, which is typically 1-2 business days.

6.6 Auctions & Bidding

Gear may support fixed-price ("Buy Now") listings, auction listings (bidding only), and hybrid listings (Buy Now + bidding). The availability of each option depends on the listing configuration chosen by the seller.

  • Hybrid fairness: For hybrid listings, the Buy Now option is disabled once the first bid is placed.
  • Anti-sniping: If a valid bid is placed within the final 5 minutes of an auction, the auction end time may automatically extend by 5 minutes. Total extensions are capped at 60 minutes.
  • Reserve price: Some auctions may have a reserve price. Gear may indicate whether a reserve has been met, but does not display the reserve amount.
Auction payment window (48 hours):

If you win an auction, you must complete payment within 48 hours of the auction ending (or by the deadline shown in the App). If payment is not completed within this window, Gear may cancel the order, notify the seller, and the item may be relisted or made available to other buyers.

Gear may take account action for repeated non-payment or other misuse, including limiting bidding features, holding payouts, or suspending accounts.
