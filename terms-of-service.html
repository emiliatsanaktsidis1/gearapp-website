If payout setup is not completed within this window, the order will be automatically cancelled and the buyer will be refunded.

6.2 Marketplace Fee

Gear charges a 10% marketplace fee on the total sale amount (item price plus shipping). This fee is automatically deducted from your payout. For example, if you sell an item for $100 with $10 shipping, your payout will be $99 (90% of $110).

6.3 Payout Holds & Release Schedule

To protect buyers and maintain marketplace integrity, seller payouts are held until fulfilment is confirmed. Payouts may also be subject to additional verification checks by Stripe or Gear before release.

  • Tracked shipments: Payout is released 3 business days after the tracking shows delivered. If tracking does not show delivered within 14 days, the payout will be released automatically.
  • Untracked shipments: Payout is released 10 business days after the seller marks the order as shipped.

Business days exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and are calculated based on Australian Eastern Time.

6.4 Disputes and Refunds

If an order is disputed or refunded before the payout is released, the pending payout will be voided. Gear reserves the right to hold or delay payouts if there is suspected fraud, policy violations, or ongoing disputes.

6.5 Payout Processing

Once a payout is released, it will be transferred to your connected Stripe account. The time it takes to reach your bank account depends on your bank and Stripe's processing times, which is typically 1–2 business days.
