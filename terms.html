Gear provides a platform for users to list, buy, and sell car parts. Gear does not own, inspect, or guarantee any items listed on the App. Any contract of sale is strictly between the buyer and the seller.

Gear charges a 10% marketplace fee on the total sale amount, including item price and shipping. This fee is deducted at the time of sale and is non-refundable.

Payments are processed by Stripe. Gear does not store complete credit card numbers and receives only limited payment data and tokens necessary to facilitate orders and payouts.

  • Gear is not responsible for lost, delayed, or damaged shipments.

7. Refunds & Returns

All sales are final except where an item is significantly not as described.
